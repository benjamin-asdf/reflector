Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Overview

Reflector is a simple utility app that changes its screen color based on your device's accelerometer. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

Reflector does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All accelerometer sensor data is processed locally on your device in real time and is never sent to any server.

Sensor Data

The app accesses your device's accelerometer solely to determine screen color. This data is used only while the app is open and is not recorded or persisted in any way.

Third-Party Services

Reflector does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Reflector does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's repository.
